Visit Margarita Island

Enjoy beach and food in this tropical island located north of Venezuela.

Top three activities to do at Margarita Island

Visit the beach

Margarita Island beaches are beautiful with crystal clear waters and leafy palm trees.

Boat ride on the lagoon

La Restinga National Park is a particular lagoon formed mainly by mangroves.

Delicious gastronomy

The products of the sea are the basis of the gastronomy of the island
